# Sales data analysis project
Analyzed 10,000+ sales records using Python (Pandas) and SQL to uncover key insights on revenue trends, customer behaviour, and branch performance.

### Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/najir0123/walmart-10k-sales-datasets

# Dataset Overview
- The Walmart Sales dataset contains 10000 records across 100 branches, including invoice_id, City, category, unit_price , quantity, date, time, payment_method, rating, profit_margin.
- Each row represents an individual sales transaction with details on product category, pricing, quantity sold, payment behaviour, date, customer rating, profit margin, and timestamp information.

# Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas) – Data Cleaning & Pre-processing
- MySQL – Data Analysis & Queries

# Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Walmart’s sales data to uncover insights about sales performance, customer preferences, and operational patterns across different branches and product categories. Using Python for data cleaning and pre-processing, and MySQL for analytical queries, the project evaluates key metrics such as revenue, profit, ratings, payment behaviour, sales seasonality, and store performance. The project aims to provide actionable insights supporting business decision-making, inventory planning, and marketing strategies.

# Data Insights
### 1. Strong Profitability Overall
- The business generated a total revenue of $1.21M with a solid profit of $476K, indicating a healthy profit margin and efficient cost management.
 
### 2. Credit Card Drives Higher Sales Volume
- Credit card users contribute the most transactions (4256) and purchase the highest quantity (9567), suggesting customers using credit cards tend to spend more and make larger purchases.

### 3. Highest Rated Category per Branch
- For all 100 branches, the top-rated category was identified based on average customer rating. This helps highlight which product categories perform best in terms of customer satisfaction at each branch.

### 4. Monthly Sales Trend
- December recorded the highest sales amount, totalling 252,617.00, indicating strong seasonal demand during the end-of-year period.

### 5. Busiest Day by Branch
- Each branch has a specific peak weekday where the number of transactions is highest. This helps understand weekly demand patterns for each Branch. 

### 6. Category-Level Performance
- Fashion Accessories recorded the highest performance with 4,538 total sales, ₹489,480.90 revenue, and ₹192,314.89 profit, making it the key revenue-driving category.

### 7. Customer Rating Distribution
- For each Branch, City, and Category, the average, minimum, and maximum ratings were calculated.
- This helps identify which locations and categories deliver the best customer experience.

### 8. Most Common Payment Method by Branch
- Each branch has a preferred payment method, with one method being the most frequently used. This insight helps tailor local payment offers or promotions.
### 9. Revenue Contribution Percentage by Branch
- Each branch’s percentage contribution to total revenue was calculated. This shows which branches are key revenue drivers for the business.

### 10. Peak Sales Hour per Branch
- Every branch has a specific hour of the day with the highest sales volume, helping optimize staffing and promotional activities during peak hours.

### 11. Most Profitable Category in Each City
- For each city, the category generating the highest total profit was identified, helping guide city-level inventory and marketing strategies.

### 12. Month-over-Month Sales Trend by Category
- Sales trends were analyzed month-by-month for each category to identify seasonality patterns, growth trends, and categories that fluctuate during different months.
### 13. Fluctuating Year-over-Year Growth Trend
- Revenue shows an inconsistent trend with declines in 2020 (-28.66%) and 2022
(-6.71%), followed by recovery in 2021 (6.21%) and 2023 (6.83%), indicating instability and potential external or seasonal impacts on performance.
### 14. Year-over-Year Growth per Branch
- Annual comparisons for each branch were performed to measure year-over-year revenue growth, identifying branches with improving or declining performance.
